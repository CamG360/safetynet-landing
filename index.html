WHY SAFETYNET?

The simple reasons people choose SafetyNet

One clear safety alert when something might be wrong

Your friend only receives a safety alert if there’s a potential issue — not reminders or constant updates. No noise, no spam — just one clear signal when you don’t confirm you are safe.

Critical info, exactly when needed

Add private plans and context — who you’re meeting, where you’re staying, when you should be home. This Critical Safety Info is only shared with your friend if something might be wrong.

Proactive protection, even when you can’t reach your phone

If you don’t confirm you are safe by your Safe Time, SafetyNet sends an alert from our servers — even if your phone is off, dead, or out of reach.

No tracking — privacy until safety is at risk

SafetyNet doesn’t track your live location. Nothing is shared while you go about your day; information is only sent if you don’t confirm you are safe on time.

Takes under a minute, then runs in the background

Set a Safe Time in under a minute. After that, simply tap “I’m Safe” when you’re done — no ongoing setup, no heavy configuration.

You stay in control

Choose who gets alerted, customise the message in your own words, and set a grace period that fits your routine before any alert is sent.

Setup: 3 simple steps (under 1 min)

Step 1

Set your Safe Time

The time by which you would expect to be safe.

Example: Will be home by 22:00

You can change your Safe Time as life changes.

Step 2

Customise your Safety Alert message

Quickly set out what you're doing and where.

Example: Last minute running date with James (Tinder) around the Lake. Call me if you get this message.

Your customised message allows the recipient to understand your specific situation and exactly what to do.

Step 3

Enter your friend's contact details

Usually your emergency contact.

Example: The person you might "text when you're safe"

Your Friend would receive the Safety Alert in the event that it is triggered, along with the customised message.

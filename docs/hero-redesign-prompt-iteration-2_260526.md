# Claude.ai/Design Prompt — Hero Redesign Iteration 2

**Date:** 2026-05-26
**Scope:** Hero section only

---

Update the hero section only. Do not change any other section.

**Remove**
- The headline "Your silent safeguard for everyday life"
- The sub-hero explanation section below the hero
- The animated scan rings
- The monitoring strip ("System active · monitoring", UTC clock)
- The registration marks (corner brackets)
- The "dead man's switch" mono label

**Hero composition**
Full-bleed image of a real person in a real situation — a woman on a first date at a bar, or walking home alone at night. Dark, cinematic, intimate. The image fills the entire hero.

Overlaid on the image, centred or lower-third, a phone UI card showing three status lines:
- "Check-in by: 11:30 PM"
- "Connected to emergency contacts"
- "Automatic alert ready"

This card should feel like a real app interface, not a decoration.

**Copy over the image**
One line, white, Inter:
> "Text me when you're safe" fails when you can't text. SafetyNet sends an alert for you.

Below that, a single CTA button: **Get Early Access**

**Typography**
Inter throughout. No serif. No monospace.

**Aesthetic**
Minimal. Dark image, white text, one card, one button. Nothing else in the hero. No badges, no trust rows, no scroll indicators, no secondary actions.
